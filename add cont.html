Best Souvenirs unique to Sri Lanka


Travaling to sri lanaka is your best choice


Most popular Souvenirs

Traditional Masks

The key feature of Sri Lankan folklore and a striking characteristic of the island's culture. Local artisans in this seaside hamlet use their skilled hands to give a simple log life and build fascinating masks with detailed characteristics.

Ceylon Tea

Ceylon tea is the island country's main export, and there are tea shops everywhere. Among the high-end and rare teas that are offered are Flowery Broken Orange Pekoe Fannings, Silver Tips, and Golden Tips.

Porcelain Tableware

Because to its wealth of minerals and clay, Sri Lanka creates exquisite porcelain and ceramic ware. In several regions of the globe, it is common to see teacups, bowls, and even silverware in simple and attractive designs.

Sri Lankan Paintings

Sri Lankan artwork can give your walls a mystical feel. The paintings, which are the works of the many outstanding painters in the nation, show festivities, processions, and other images that have a strong local flavor.

Gems

Sri Lanka is referred to as the Gem Isle since it is a country that engages in gem mining and has a remarkable collection of gems that have been discovered.

Sri Lankan Wood Figurines

Sri Lankans are renowned for producing exquisite figures from materials including ebony, stone, and wood. The most well-known of them are the finely carved Buddha and elephant sculptures.
